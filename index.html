About MaxCafe Scheveningen

MaxCafe was founded by Bert Muskitta and Sammy de Fretes in June 2015. Located in the vibrant harbour of Scheveningen, we cater to music and food lovers across all ages.

We have a healthy and affordable menu and a wide variety of quality drinks for you to enjoy. Every Sunday we have an Open Mic from 12pm-3pm and a booked artist/band from 3pm-5pm. Make sure to like our Facebook page and follow our events.

FAQs

Have any food allergies?
No problem for us! A quick word with the chef and we will come up with a creative alternative for you.

Is there a kid's corner?
Yes, we have a selection of toys and crayons for your little ones to play with.

Are my pets welcome?
Yes, we make sure your furry friends are welcome. We also have a water bowl available for when they're thirsty!

What about parking?
Yes, we have FREE parking available.

Does the jukebox work?
Oh yes, it does! Just ask Sammy if you want to play a song on it.
